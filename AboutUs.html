About Us

Friendly is a platform dedicated to help people on a global scale, in their journeys to sustainability. Our website and App, make it possible for everyday people to track their carbon footprint, and contributions to a cleaner environment. The platform completes this by calculating how much waste a person produces daily, as well as the amount of items recycled by said person. In addition, a portion of our proceeds go to shelters to provide food and clothing for those who are homeless or less fortunate.
