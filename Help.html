Commonly Asked Questions:

How do I navigate the website?

Simply click on any of the categories listed at the top of our homepage, and each will lead you to a new page.

How do I track my daily waste or recycles?

Whenever you would like to track your daily waste or track my recycles:

Step 1: Log into your page

Step 2: Click on the Track Cycle category listed at the top of our page

Step 3: Click on the "track my daily waste" button or "track my daily recycles" button, depending on your need. Use the log to record your daily wastes (ex. using a plastic bag) or recycles (ex. recycling a water bottle)
